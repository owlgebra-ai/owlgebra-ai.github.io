GEPA

Generalized Evolutionary Policy Adaptation.

Evolve neural policies across heterogeneous environments using population-based search, novelty metrics, and gradient-free fitness optimization.

View on GitHub
A
Population-Based Search

Maintain diverse populations of policies and apply evolutionary operators — mutation, crossover, selection.

B
Novelty-Driven Exploration

Navigate sparse reward landscapes by rewarding behavioral novelty alongside task performance.

C
Cross-Environment Generalization

Policies trained via GEPA transfer better across environments with shifted dynamics and objectives.

D
Gradient-Free Optimization

Evolve black-box models and non-differentiable systems where gradient methods fail.
